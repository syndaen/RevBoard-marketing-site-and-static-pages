Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your RevBoard account and all associated data.

RevBoard lets you delete your account entirely from within the app. Deletion is immediate and permanent — once confirmed, your garage, builds, modifications, photos, reputation, check-in history, profile data, and account credentials are all removed. This action cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open RevBoard and navigate to the Profile tab (bottom-right).
  2. Scroll down and open the Account & Security section.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Read the confirmation prompt, then tap Confirm to permanently delete your account.

After confirmation, you will be signed out immediately and your data will begin permanent deletion.

Data Retention

Account deletion takes effect immediately upon confirmation. All data listed above is removed from RevBoard's production database at the time of deletion.

Encrypted database backups may retain a transient copy of deleted data for up to 7 days as part of RevBoard's disaster recovery infrastructure. These backups are not accessible to any user or staff member and are automatically purged on a rolling schedule. No deleted data is retained beyond this backup window.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to sign in or access the deletion flow within RevBoard, you can request account deletion by email.

Email: support@getrevboard.com

Include the email address associated with your RevBoard account. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 48 hours.
